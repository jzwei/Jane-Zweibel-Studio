Hello! My name is Jane and I am a multidisciplinary artist living in Philadelphia with my partner and our elderly dog Frances. I have an MS in design and have experience in many media including fashion design, ceramics, jewelry making, graphic design, and much more-- but my studio work focuses on acrylic painting on canvas. I paint to express feelings of delight and affection through energetic layers of bright and playful shapes and patterns.

I began painting in 2020 with my mom after she pivoted her work from exclusively ceramics and surface design into painting, and encouraged me to join her. Now after nearly 8 years working at the Anthropologie headquarters developing and producing hard & soft-goods products for the Anthro Living (Home & Furniture) division I have left my job at the beginning of this year to pursue work as an artist full time.
