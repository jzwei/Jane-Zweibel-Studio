ABOUT

Jane Zweibel is a multidisciplinary artist whose studio work focuses on acrylic paintings which express feelings of delight and affection through energetic layers of bright and playful shapes and patterns.

Jane is based in Philadelphia where she is a textile production manager, web developer, and curly dog enthusiast with an MS in design. She has lived in Philadelphia since 2009, and has spent the last seven years working at the Anthropologie headquarters developing and producing hard & soft-goods products for the Anthro Living (Home & Furniture) division.
