I am Jane

And I'm an Artist

Jane Zweibel is a multidisciplinary artist whose studio work focuses on acrylic paintings which express feelings of delight and affection through energetic layers of bright and playful shapes and patterns. Jane is based in Philadelphia where she works as an artist, web developer, and curly dog enthusiast. She has an MS in design and has previously worked for 8 years at the Anthropologie headquarters developing and producing hard & soft-goods products for the Anthro Living (Home & Furniture) division.
